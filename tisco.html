ABOUT US

The TEYSEER GROUP has a history of over four decades of business activities.

Teyseer Industrial Supplies & Services Company W.L.L. (TISSCO), member of Teyseer Group, established in the year 1999 as an industrial supplies and services company. TISSCO, an ISO 9001:2008 certified, is focused on supplies and services of Electro Mechanical Industrial Products, in Qatar.

TISSCO is one of the major suppliers contributing to The Qatari industrial, projects and trader segment growth through twelve (12) marketing / product oriented divisions. Major products handled by TISSCO includes Machineries, Tools, Electrical Products, Oil & Gas Field Supplies, Safety & Security Products, All types of Hoses, Hardware & Building material products, Power Tools, Hydraulic Products etc. etc.

Service is a vital activity of TISSCO. This include Calibration & Certification Services (a Joint Venture with a U.S Calibration Laboratory ‚Äì Microprecision Laboratories), Hydraulic Services, Power Tool Services, Fire Extinguisher Services, Hose Assembly and Equipment & Machinery Services. Our scope of service activities covers on-site, off-site and mobile services.

TISSCO represents world renowned international brands such as STANLEY BLACK & DECKER, FACOM, EGA MASTER, HOLMATRO, MOBIAK S.A, SCHNEIDER ELECTRIC, KNIPEX, EXTECH INTRUMENTS, FLEET GUARD, PIUSI, YOUNG MAN, etc...

SERVICES

Hose Assembly Services

FTC

This service provides complete fabrication and assembly of high-quality industrial and hydraulic hoses, ensuring zero-leak operations.

Truck Assembly Services

FTC

Offering custom assembly and modification for specialized vehicles, including mobile workshops and fluid dispensing trucks.

Oil Monitoring System Services

FTC

Installation, calibration, and maintenance of lubrication and oil monitoring systems for heavy machinery and industrial assets.

Machinery Services

EMT
